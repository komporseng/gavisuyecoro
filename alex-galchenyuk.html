Alex Galchenyuk Biography

Alex Galchenyuk was born in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, to a Belarusian mother and former Soviet and Belarusian hockey player Alexander Galchenyuk. Anna is his elder sister. At the time of his birth, Galchenyuk’s father played with the Milwaukee Admirals of the International Hockey League (IHL). When Galchenyuk was four years old, his family relocated to Europe as a result of his father’s hockey career in Germany, Italy, and Russia.

When they finally settled, the younger Galchenyuk started his hockey career in Russia. When he was 15, he moved back to North America, first to Chicago, Illinois, and then to Sarnia, Ontario, where Alex Galchenyuk, Sr. is a coach. Galchenyuk is the chairman of both the Galchenyuk Agency and Galchenyuk Business Enterprises or GBE.
