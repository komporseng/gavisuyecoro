A Quick Tour Of Morris's Father's Career:

Dirk Nowitzki has numerous awards under his belt, which is also the reason behind his tremendous success. He became the ''Most Valuable Player of the NBA'' in 2017, as well as the Finals MVP in 2011. He was the champion of the Three-Point Contest in 2006.
