The Purple Nectar (Japanese: むらさきのミツ Purple Nectar) is an item introduced in Generation VII. It is one of the nectars associated with Oricorio.

In the core series games

Price

Effect

When used from the Bag on an Oricorio, it changes it to Sensu Style. It can only be used outside of battle. It is consumed after use.
