Drake Bell, born June 27, 1986, is an actor and singer, best known as the child star of Nickelodeon series like ‘The Amanda Show’ and ‘Drake & Josh.’ He started his career at just five with commercials and tv appearances before becoming involved with Nickelodeon. He’s done voice work in Nick’s ‘The Fairly OddParents’ and Disney XD’s ‘Ultimate Spider-Man’.

Drake also has a music career, releasing three albums as a solo artist. He has a huge fanbase in Latin America. 

Drake has endured some legal issues in recent years. In December 2015, he was arrested for driving under the influence. In 2021 he was arrested for ‘attempted endangering of children’, and later accused of sexual assault by the victim. Drake was sentenced to two years of probation and 200 hours of community service. As for his personal life, Drake revealed that he has been married since 2018 and has a son in July 2021.

Unfortunately, Drake was also reported missing by Florida in Daytona in a Facebook post by the department on Thursday, April 13. The statement announced that he was ‘missing and endangered.’ Hours after he was first reported missing police confirmed that he’d been found on Facebook. “At this time, we can confirm law enforcement officials are in contact and Mr. Bell is safe,” they wrote.
