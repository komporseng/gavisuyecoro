Nathaniel V. Breaux Missing – An early-dawn fire on Sunday morning caused multiple businesses in Fairhaven to be destroyed, and the authorities have been unable to identify a single person who was involved with the structure. During the evening of Sunday, Janice Keller, who works for the city of Bellingham, confirmed that Nathaniel V. Breaux, who is 39 years old, has been reported missing to the Bellingham Police Department. There is a cafe on Harris Avenue that is owned by Breaux.

Just before midnight, a number of fire engines arrived to take action against the blaze. The Harris Avenue Cafe and the Old Independent Coffee House are both located in the building, which was formerly occupied by Tony’s Coffee and is now the location of both establishments. The fire also caused the destruction of private offices that were located above the two places.

The fire caused the roof and the second floor of the structure to fall, which prevented the fire men from performing a thorough search of the building. Bellingham incident Chief Bill Hewett stated that crews entered the structure during the incident in order to search for anyone who could be inside. However, they were unable to search the second level because they noted that the floor was beginning to droop as they were searching for someone.

According to Hewett, the fire department had been in communication with the proprietors of the coffee shop and the café, but as of Sunday afternoon, they were unable to determine who the current proprietors of all of the offices on the second level were. Despite the fact that there have been no reports of injuries, the fire crews will have to wait in order to search the wreckage for anyone who may have been trapped inside and to investigate the origin of the fire themselves.

As Hewett put it, “We are very concerned about the brick that is located outside of the structure falling from its place.” “At this very moment, we are making efforts to recruit individuals to assist with securing the exterior of the building. As long as that is not completed, we will not be able to allow teams to access the facility in order to conduct a comprehensive search of the building.
