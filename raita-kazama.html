Kazama in 2023[1]

Raita Kazama (Japanese: 風間雷太 Kazama Raita) is an artist for the Pokémon series. His earliest contributions to the franchise was as a merchandise artist in 2022, drawing for the Cynthia's Rise to Action line. His work on merchandise would continue into 2023, where he would be credited as the artist behind the Shiny Lucario event artwork for Pokémon Scarlet and Violet.
